Registration Trial Period & Baseline Fees
All new customer accounts include an initial 30-day free trial phase. During this trial period, a standardized platform application booking fee of KSh 30 applies per unique booking request. Core subscription logic dictating maximum vehicle tracking limits remains enforced throughout the trial span.
Track 1 — Tier discounts
Automatic. No points required.
Your tier status alone unlocks standing discounts on add-ons and premium services. Silver: 5%. Gold: 10%. Platinum: 15%. These apply at checkout without any spend.
Track 2 — Point redemptions
Deliberate. Spend for bigger access.
Points are spent to unlock things beyond your tier discount — priority slots, early access, extended cancellation windows, partner vouchers. Points buy platform privileges, not cheaper soap.

Point expiry & tier decay
Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity (no completed wash). Tier status is reviewed every 6 months — if a customer's rolling-6-month points fall below the tier threshold by ≥30%, they are downgraded one tier. Points earned within the prior 6 months are retained. This keeps tiers meaningful and prevents "coast" behaviour.

Anti-gaming & integrity rules
01
No points on unverified completions. Points only fire after the booking_status = 'completed' flag is set server-side, triggered by wash point operator action — not by the customer. Customer self-reporting cannot award points.
02
Review gating. Reviews require a minimum 20-character text body. Star-only ratings do not earn the review bonus. Duplicate review attempts within the same booking ID are blocked at DB level.
03
Referral verification. Referral bonus (50 pts) is only credited when the referred user's first booking reaches completed status. Account sharing or self-referral (same device fingerprint, phone number, or IP) is disqualified and flagged for review.
04
No point redemptions for wash price reductions. Points cannot be applied to reduce the cost of a wash booking or basic subscription billing structures. Tier discounts (Track 1) are the only mechanism for commercial value — and they apply automatically. Points are strictly for platform privileges: access, priority, and partner vouchers.
05
Tier qualifier — minimum activity. Tier status requires the customer to have completed ≥ 1 wash in the last 90 days. Dormant accounts retain points but are demoted to Bronze regardless of point balance.
06
Washpoint rating manipulation. If a wash point's review pattern shows abnormal clustering (e.g., ≥ 5 five-star reviews from new accounts within 48 hours), SplashScore calculation pauses for manual audit. Confirmed manipulation results in Standard demotion and 60-day lockout.
07
Voucher non-transferability. Redeemed vouchers are tied to the customer's phone number / UID and cannot be gifted, sold, or used on a different account. Voucher codes are single-use with 30-day expiry.
08
Tier discounts are non-stackable. Track 1 tier discounts cannot be combined with core monthly/weekly subscription rates, promotional baseline prices, operator coupons, or any other discount in the same transaction. Only one commercial discount mechanism applies per booking. Point redemptions (Track 2) do not interact with this — they grant access, not price reductions.
09
Point balance cap. Maximum accumulation is capped at 10,000 pts at any time. Points earned beyond the cap are forfeited. This prevents hoarding and forced large-scale redemptions.
10
SplashPass reserves right to audit. Any account showing earning velocity > 3× the average for their tier segment triggers an automated flag and a 7-day earning freeze pending review. Points earned during the freeze period are held in escrow.
